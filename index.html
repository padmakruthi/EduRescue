Essential Safety Tips

🌍

Earthquake

Drop, Cover, Hold On. Stay away from windows and tall furniture.

💧

Flood

Move to higher ground immediately. Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.

🔥

Fire

Activate the fire alarm. Use exits and avoid elevators. Crawl low to avoid smoke.
